AxisCare → IRIS Provider Invoice

Upload a ClaimBatchCreation CSV. You'll get back one IRIS Provider Invoice PDF per participant per service month.

  • One invoice per participant per calendar month.
  • Same-day visits for a participant are merged into a single line.
  • Up to 9 service dates per page; longer months overflow into _pt2, _pt3, …
  • Units are reported in 15-minute increments at the equivalent unit rate.

This tool handles Protected Health Information (PHI). Uploaded files are processed entirely in memory and are not written to disk or transmitted to any third party. Do not share generated PDFs outside of authorized billing workflows.
